  • Retailers

    1. Competitive Analysis - Retailers

    As a control state, Virginia has 1/3 the liquor stores than the average US state and the 3rd highest liquor prices in the nation. In order to grow revenue, we need to maintain prices and control expenses while increasing market share, all the while proving the value of VA ABC to its stakeholders. Accomplishing these goals will take more than stealing share from beer and wine. VA ABC must utilize all competitive advantages.

    These advantages include:

    1. VA ABC supports a healthier, safer, and more prosperous Virginia. We must capitalize on the pride in our local state to form emotional connections with all audiences, including consumers, VA residents, restaurant owners, and legislatures.

    2. As a control state, the monopoly VA ABC has on liquor sales means that it has an extensive infrastructure as well as legislative influence. This framework can be used as a platform for innovation, creating solutions that allow VA ABC to compete on convenience.
